"choices by IMPLEMENTS" YouTube channel open!!

01 May 2020

We started our YouTube channel from May 1st.
The channel name is "choices by IMPLEMENTS".

choices by IMPLEMENTS

"choice" doesn't mean only choosing, but also selection/high quality and
from the perspective of studio that is involved in the design of daily life products,
we will share information on the theme of "living", "design", and "manufacturing".

Regarding design and manufacturing, we will not showcase only our projects,
but also contents about beautiful products and their creators.
Please subscribe to the channel!

choices by IMPLEMENTS
